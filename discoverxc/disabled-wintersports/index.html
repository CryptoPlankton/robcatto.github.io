Disabled snowsport programs

DWA is an organisation that collaborates with many other support and community focused organisations that come together to deliver a disabled snowsports programs. Check out Disabled Wintersport Australia for information on how to access supported xc skiing in Australia.

Winter Paralympics

There is also the chance of competing in the Winter Paralympics. Cross country skiing first appeared at the 1976 Winter Paralympic Games in Örnsköldsvik, Sweden. The competition is open to athletes with a physical impairment and blindness/visual impairment. Male and female athletes compete in short distance, middle distance and long distance (ranging from 2.5km to 20km) or participate in a team relay using classical or free techniques. Biathlon is also an event at the Paralympics.
