University

Never too old to learn how to xc ski

The timing of university holidays is perfect for the snow season in Australia and overseas. Check out whether your uni has a ski club and meet other keen xc skiers. There are the Australian University Championships to join in - racing for some, fun for others. Go overseas and participate in the World University Winter Games. An experience you will not forget.

You might get a chance to ski with a National Team members as most are aged 17-21 with many combining uni with a training schedule. There are also various summer working and skiing opportunities worldwide to break up your uni year.
