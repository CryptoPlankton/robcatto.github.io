Backcountry

Find the untracked slopes!

Backcountry skiing is when skiers head outside the ski resort boundaries to find untracked slopes. Backcountry skiers seek solitude, freedom and the challenge of finding fresh unexplored snow.

What techniques do you use in backcountry

Backcountry skiers use telemark turns or lock in their boots and use standard downhill techniques to negotiate a slope.

For the more advanced skier

This type of skiing is for the more advanced skier who had lots of experience in the alpine environment. Most skiers get into this after doing a specialised course in backcountry skiing or by joining a club and meeting other keen backcountry skiers.
