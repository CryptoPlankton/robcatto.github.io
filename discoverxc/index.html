Classic and Skating - What's the difference?

Cross country skiing involves two different styles. In Classic style the skis stay parallel most of the time and a grip section on the bottom of the skis stops it from gliding backwards. What type of XC Skiing would suit me?

Classic in a resort on groomed trails is usually the best starting point for most beginners. By combining this with ski play, to improve agility and balance on skis, beginners can rapidly progress. And with good balance and weight transfer skiers can progress quickly to skating. From there you can explore the options outside the resort including, ski touring out to a hut or even do some backcountry skiing on untracked snow. Perhaps once you have conquered skating try some Spring Skiing out on the Bogong High Plains!
