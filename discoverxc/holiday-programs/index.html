Holiday Programs

Experience the beautiful Bogong High Plains whilst skiing the Joey Hoppet

NSW dryland and on snow School Holiday Program

The NSWXC School Holiday program runs over the 4 week duration of the NSW/ACT School Holidays at Perisher on-snow or around Jindabyne if there is minimal/no snow. Up to 12 days of training is included in the NSWXC Squad and Team membership and up to 12 half-days in the NSWXC Club membership. Groups of 6-10 with roughly equal ability work with coaches from diverse backgrounds in order to develop skiing specific skills, balance, technique and fitness.

Other holiday programs

Club/community groups also run holiday programs for members which are very popular. Also talk your your local XC ski school to see if there are any holiday programs being run.
