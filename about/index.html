Get inspired to try xc skiing!

DISCOVERXCSKI.com was a developed with the main aim of increasing grassroots particiapation in XC skiing in Australia. It is an introductory information portal for cross country skiing in Australia. Where you can explore the world of cross country skiing, get inspired by what it can offer and most importantly get motivated to join a growing xc community. The main drivers of the project Rob Catto-Smith, Kate Prest and Finn Marsland hope to see you on xc skis in the very near future!
