What is Racing?

XC racing is fun. Races are either short fast races called sprint races or longer distance races. Most races are freestyle where you can skate or classic, but some are purely just classic. There is race to suit you!

Racing in Australia

In Australia races fall into three main categories. Club races, which are open to all to participants from under 5 to over 80. Championship races, where you can win a state or national title and qualify for National Team level competition. And finally schools events, which are open to any primary or secondary school students.

Biathlon, a Combination of Target Shooting and Racing

Biathlon could be right up your alley. Biathlon is a winter sport that is fun, challenging and exciting. It combines cross-country freestyle skiing and target shooting. A great way to expand what you can do on xc skis.
