Discover Racing

Racing is for everyone of all skiing ability

Don’t get put off by the term ‘racing’. Racing is actually for everyone!  From young to old. Whether you are a beginner, intermediate or advanced skier you can benefit from racing. It’s a great chance to meet new people, make new friends and improve your technique and fitness.

Combine target shooting and racing

Biathlon is a winter sport that is fun, challenging and exciting. It combines cross-country skate skiing and small-bore target shooting. A great way to expand what you can do on xc skis!
