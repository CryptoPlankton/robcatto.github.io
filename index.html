    Racing is for everyone of any skiing ability

    Racing is actually for everyone! From young to old. Whether you are a beginner, intermediate or advanced skier you can benefit from racing. It’s a great chance to meet new people, make new friends and improve your technique and fitness.

    What types of races are there?

    There are heaps of race types in both classic and skate skiing. Whether you like really short fast races or prefer to pace yourself there will definitely be something for you. You can read about the different race types below.

    Racing "Groups" in Australia

    In Australia there are three main categories. Club races, which are open to all to participants from under 5 to over 80. Championship races, where you can win a state or national title and qualify for National Team level competition and Schools events, which are open to any primary or secondary school students.

    What is Ausxc?

    Ausxc.com provides more detail and advice on the racing side of xc skiing and is the home of XC Skiing in Australia. If you want to find out about news, results and athletes check out AUSXC.
