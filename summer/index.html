Summer

Not just a Winter Sport!

There are various off-snow equivalents to cross country skiing on offer in Australia. These include rollerskiing, skiking or nordic walking. All of them are great year round activity for maintaining fitness, working on technique, or even learning to ski before the winter.

Get involved in activities and events throughout summer

Once you get confident on rollerskis and skikes there are dryland events such as the Victorian Rollerski/Skike Championships to get involved in. There are also lots of training/social groups for rollerskiers, skikers and nordic walkers in many locations around Australia. Find out more by checking out the events calendar, talking to your local community group/club or retailer.

Chase the Endless Winter

What do XC skiers do after the snow melts? Of course you can get into rollerskiing, skiking, nordic walking or just get back on the bike. But if you’ve caught the snow bug and can’t wait until the next Australian winter check out the overseas winter options.

All around the world! You can represent Australia at the World Masters Cup or ski the Worldloppet events. And if you are interested in becoming an elite athlete have a look at where racing can take you!

Biathlon, a Combination of Target Shooting and Racing

Biathlon could be right up your alley. Biathlon is a winter sport that is fun, challenging and exciting. It combines cross-country freestyle skiing and target shooting. A great way to expand what you can do on xc skis.
