ACT Schools Cup

This event is for ACT school students to race each other as individuals and/or part of a team of 3. The event is run similar to Interschools where students compete in their Interschools division (year at school) and the top 3 teams and individuals are recognised. Beginner Social & Race
Perisher Saturday 6 August

Perisher XC Ski Week

Do you want plenty of skiing, fresh air and fun for a whole week? Try Perisher XC Ski Week. Not just a series of individual events but now one big event. XC Ski Week is a week that caters for all levels of skiers whether you take part in the events or be a part of the cheering crowd. If you feel like racing, it is purely up to yourself as to how fast or slow you want to go. All participants are cheered on and skiers new to each event are especially welcome. Events include racing, Mystery Coffee Tours, try Laser Biathlon, Fast & Female (girls only!), demonstrations and waxing clinics, plus all the free skiing that you can handle.

More Info
Intermediate Race
Perisher Wednesday 10 August

KAC X-C Classic

The KAC race is a key race in the Perisher race calendar and part of Perisher XC Ski Week. The race is from Perisher Valley to Charlotte Pass Village, approximately 8 km. The classic skiers start first, followed 15 minutes later on the same course, by the freestyle skiers. Skiers must nominate on the entry form which style they wish to enter. Immediately following the race there will be a party in Charlotte Pass village. There will be free food, drink and spot prizes in conjunction with the prize giving. Come and enjoy!
