Join a Club

A great way learn and be part of the XC community

There are a number of ski clubs and community groups that will support you in discovering cross country skiing. Check them all out as it depends on what type of xc skiing you are interested in. Many of them have free events open to everyone. Joining a community is a great way of making friends and being part of the wider XC community.
