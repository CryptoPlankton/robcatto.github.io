What you need to know to go and try XC!

Australia has a number of ski resorts to visit that offer great XC skiing. Whether you rent or buy, you must be geared up correctly with suitable clothing and equipment to enjoy your xc skiing experience. There are also lots of other things to consider when planning a trip including wax, weather and your safety.

Not just a Winter Sport!

There are various off-snow equivalents to cross country skiing on offer in Australia. These include rollerskiing, skiking or nordic walking. All of them are great year round activity for maintaining fitness, working on technique, or even learning to ski before the winter.
