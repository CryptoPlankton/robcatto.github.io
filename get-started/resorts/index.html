Where to ski in Australia

Australia has a number of ski resorts to explore that offer XC skiing opportunities. The main five when it comes to XC trail grooming are Falls Creek, Perisher, Lake Mountain, Mt Hotham and Mt Stirling, but there can also be groomed trails at the other resorts. When planning your trip to the snow select a resort or area which caters for you and your group's needs, as facilities, experiences and opportunities can vary widely.
