Weather

Be prepared for all conditions

Mountain weather can be extremely unpredictable and skiers are advised to be prepared for all conditions and check weather forecasts before setting out. It will allow you to choose the correct clothing, decide which skis to take and decide where you should ski that day. Also it is best to try and choose a clear sunny day for your first xc skiing experience.

There are a number of different weather and snow forcasting websites which you can check out the weather and snow conditions before you arrive. These include Bureau of Meteorology, Jane's Weather, Mountain Watch, and ski.com.au.
