XC Ski Schools

A great way to get started

Lessons through a ski school are a great way to get started cross country skiing and shorten the learning curve. Do not expect too much on your first attempt at skiing but after your first go you should improve quickly to a basic level. Further lessons will refine your technique. Even the really good skiers and athletes have lessons to improve their techniques. Most larger ski resorts have ski schools that specialise in cross country skiing.
